Sunburst charts

Sunburst charts are used to visualize categorical data composition.

The sunburst chart is an great choice to display data conposition in a compelling and effective visualization.
The demo below visualizes 27 feelings that derive from the core happy emotion.
Even though a sunburst chatrt indicates compostiion where all the value added up to 100%, nevertheless, when it comes to categorical data we can brake the rule as the data we have now are qualitative instead of being quantitative data.

Good to know

  • Use sunburst in a dashboard and small screens. Nevertheless, be sure not to overload the chart with many categories; otherwise, the audience will face challenges to see and distinguish between the periphery's arcs.
