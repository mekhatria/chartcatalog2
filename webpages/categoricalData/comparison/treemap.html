Treemap charts

Treempas are used to compare categorical data.

The demo displays the areas of the Canadian regions and provinces. Treemap chart makes it easy to see that the northern provinces have the most significant surface area than the rest of the regions. The Atlantic provinces have the smallest surface area in Canada.

Good to know

  • A treemap is an appealing and fashionable chart. Nevertheless, in some cases, a treemap could not be the best choice if the data set used for comparison have pretty close values. In this case, the audience will have difficulty comparing two areas or more that have roughly the same surface. To solve this issue, either select a different chart type such as a bar or column chart or add labels to the treemap to help the audience.
